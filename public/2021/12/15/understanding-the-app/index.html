Understanding the app

2021-12-15

Semesters’ choice

There are three tabs, for the three semesters of classes of the Master.
You can navigate across them by simply clicking on it at the top of the page.

Initial timetable suggestion

To display an initial timetable, click on “Suggest” on the sidebar panel.

You have the choice to either view, by moving across tabs in the main panel:

  • two visual representations of the selected classes (half-semester classes):

    • one for the first seven weeks of the semester

    • one for the last seven weeks of the semester

  • a textual summary of the selected classes

Note that the mandatory classes of this master are pre-selected and will be included in the suggested timetable even if de-selected them from the list of preferred classes.

User’s preferences

You can of course also input your preferences to have a personalized timetable:

  • how many credits you want to do in the Business Analytics track (core credits) ?

  • how many credits you want to do as elective classes (non mandatory ones) ?

  • which half-days you want to have class ?

Issues

Note that if no timetable is displayed, it simply mean your preferences could not be satisfied.
In this case, you need to either choose more days and remove conflicting classes.

A good advice is always to start from the first generated timetable.
Otherwise, to only add a few favorite classes and then scale it up.
